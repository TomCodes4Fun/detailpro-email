Notifications

Email templates and automation

Automation

When enabled, the app will open your email client with a pre-filled message when key events happen (confirmations, ready notices, receipts).

Tip: If the customer has no email saved, the message will be copied to your clipboard instead.

Send automatically (no popup)

By default, emails open in your own mail app for you to review and send. Turn this on to send automatically through the included email server instead — see server/ for setup.

Templates

Use placeholders like {{customerName}}, {{date}}, {{time}}, {{service}}, {{total}}, {{invoiceNumber}}.
